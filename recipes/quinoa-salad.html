Quinoa Salad

Cookie and Kate Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas
  • 1 medium cucumber, chopped
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, chopped
  • ¾ cup chopped red onion (from 1 small red onion)
  • 1 cup finely chopped flat-leaf parsley (from 1 large bunch)
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup lemon juice (from 2 to 3 lemons)
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
  • ½ teaspoon fine sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Combine rinsed quinoa and water in a medium saucepan. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat. Simmer, uncovered, until the quinoa has absorbed all the water (about 15 minutes). Remove from heat, cover, and let rest for 5 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cucmber, bell pepper, onion, and parsley.
  3. In a small bowl, combine and whisk olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, garlic, and salt.
  4. Add quinoa to serving bowl, and drizzle the dressing on top. Toss until the mixture is thoroughly combined. Season with black pepper, to taste, and salt if necessary. Let rest 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
  5. Serve chilled or at room temperature
