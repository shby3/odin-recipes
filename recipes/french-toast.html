French Toast

Felu Low-Cal French Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 slices sandwich bread
  • 1 egg
  • 150g egg whites
  • 100g milk
  • 10-20g sweetener
  • salt, cinnamon

Steps

  1. Toast bread slices on pan at medium-high heat.
  2. Add egg, egg whites, milk, sweetener, and a pinch of salt and cinnamon to a bowl. Mix until combined
  3. Remove bread from pan and submerge each slice in the liquid.
  4. Add bread back to pan. Drizzle leftover liquid into the pan. Flip slices every 30 seconds and cook until you are happy with the color.
